Services

Service 01

Receipt Scanning & Organization

Your receipts, finally under control. We scan every receipt you provide and organize them by vendor, then by date — making it effortless to locate any transaction at a glance.

  • Physical or digital receipt intake
  • Organized by vendor name
  • Sub-sorted chronologically within each vendor
  • Digital archive delivery
Service 02

Scanning + CSV Detail Entry

Everything in Service 01, plus each receipt item is individually entered into a structured CSV file following standard bookkeeping practices. A clean, importable spreadsheet ready for your accountant or software.

  • All scanning and vendor/date organization from Service 01
  • Line-item entry into CSV format
  • Standard-practice column headers (Date, Vendor, Category, Amount, Notes)
  • Ready for import into QuickBooks, Excel, or any accounting tool
Service 03

Full Bookkeeping

Comprehensive bookkeeping for your business. We handle your books with accuracy and care so you can focus on what you do best. Services are tailored to your needs and billed at our hourly rate — also available for other financial projects.

  • Income and expense tracking
  • Account reconciliation
  • Financial reports on request
  • Other project-based financial services available

About Me

RBH

Welcome to Receipt Box Hero Bookkeeping — where the chaos of shoebox receipts meets its match.

I started this business because I've seen firsthand how small business owners lose time, money, and peace of mind over disorganized financial records. A missed receipt here, an uncategorized expense there — it adds up fast.

My mission is simple: give every business owner, freelancer, and entrepreneur access to clean, organized, and accurate financial records — without the big-firm price tag.

Whether you need a one-time scan of a year's worth of receipts or ongoing bookkeeping support, I bring the same care and precision to every job. Your records matter. Let's get them right.
